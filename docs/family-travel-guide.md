# Family Travel Guide

Borderly's family profile feature allows you to manage travel information for up to 8 family members on a single device, making group travel forms faster and more convenient while keeping everyone's data secure and private.

## Overview

Family profiles in Borderly allow you to:
- Add family members (spouse, children, parents, siblings, or other travel companions)
- Securely store each person's passport information separately
- Auto-fill forms for multiple travelers on family trips
- Switch between profiles easily during form completion
- Maintain privacy with individual data encryption for each family member

## Getting Started with Family Profiles

### Adding Your First Family Member

1. **Navigate to Family Management**
   - From the main screen, tap the "Profile" tab
   - Select "Family Members" from the profile menu
   - You'll see your primary profile listed first

2. **Add a Family Member**
   - Tap the "Add Member" button
   - Choose the relationship (Spouse, Child, Parent, Sibling, or Other)
   - Add their passport information using either:
     - **Camera scan**: Scan their passport's Machine Readable Zone (MRZ)
     - **Manual entry**: Type in their passport details manually

3. **Complete Profile Setup**
   - Fill in any missing information
   - Review the details for accuracy
   - Tap "Save Family Member"

### Supported Family Relationships

- **Self**: Your primary profile (cannot be deleted)
- **Spouse**: Your spouse or partner
- **Child**: Your children (minor or adult)
- **Parent**: Your parents
- **Sibling**: Your brothers or sisters
- **Other**: Other family members or travel companions

## Managing Family Profiles

### Viewing Family Members

The Family Management screen shows all family members with:
- Name and relationship
- Passport number and expiry date
- Passport status (Valid, Expiring Soon, Expired)
- Last updated date

### Editing Family Members

- **Primary Profile**: Tap "Edit" to modify your own information
- **Family Members**: Edit functionality for additional family members is coming soon

### Removing Family Members

1. Find the family member you want to remove
2. Tap the "Remove" button on their profile card
3. Confirm the deletion in the popup
4. **Note**: This permanently deletes their data from your device

## Family Travel Planning

### Creating a Family Trip

1. **Start a New Trip**
   - Go to the "Trips" tab
   - Tap "Create Trip"
   - Enter trip name, dates, and destination(s)

2. **Select Travelers**
   - In the "Select Travelers" section, choose which family members are traveling
   - You can select any combination of family members
   - The app will generate forms for each selected traveler

3. **Country-Specific Forms**
   - Forms are automatically generated for each family member
   - Switch between travelers using the profile selector
   - Each person's information is auto-filled from their profile

### Completing Forms for Multiple Travelers

1. **Access Trip Forms**
   - Open your family trip
   - Select the destination country
   - You'll see a traveler selector at the top

2. **Switch Between Travelers**
   - Use the traveler selector to switch between family members
   - Forms are automatically populated with each person's data
   - Complete any country-specific questions for each traveler

3. **Submit Individual Forms**
   - Complete and submit forms for each family member separately
   - Each person gets their own QR codes and submission confirmations
   - Store all QR codes in the shared wallet for easy access

## Security and Privacy

### Data Isolation

- **Separate Encryption**: Each family member's passport data is stored in separate, encrypted keychain entries
- **Unique Keys**: Every family member has their own encryption key
- **Access Control**: The primary profile holder manages access to family member data

### Privacy Protection

- **Local Storage Only**: All family data stays on your device - nothing is sent to servers
- **Biometric Protection**: Use Face ID, Touch ID, or device PIN to protect access
- **Backup Exclusion**: Family data is excluded from iCloud/Google backups for security

### Data Management

- **Complete Deletion**: Removing a family member permanently deletes all their data
- **Migration Safety**: Adding family members preserves existing single-user data
- **No Data Sharing**: Family members cannot see each other's passport data

## Tips for Family Travel

### Passport Management

- **Check Expiry Dates**: The app warns when passports are expiring within 6 months
- **Update Regularly**: Keep passport information current, especially after renewals
- **Child Passports**: Remember that children's passports expire more frequently

### Trip Organization

- **Name Trips Clearly**: Use descriptive names like "Summer Europe 2024" for easy identification
- **Plan Ahead**: Add family members before creating trips for smoother form completion
- **QR Code Management**: Use the wallet feature to organize submission QR codes by family member

### Form Completion Strategy

1. **Complete Primary Profile First**: Fill out your own forms to understand requirements
2. **Review Family Data**: Double-check family member information before submitting
3. **Submit Together**: Complete all family member forms in one session when possible
4. **Save QR Codes**: Ensure all submission QR codes are saved to the wallet

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Cannot add family member"**
- Check that you haven't reached the 8-member limit
- Ensure the passport information is complete and valid
- Try restarting the app if the issue persists

**"Family member not appearing in trip selection"**
- Verify the family member profile is complete and saved
- Check that they have valid passport information
- Refresh the trip creation screen

**"Forms not auto-filling"**
- Confirm the family member's profile has all required information
- Check that you've selected the correct traveler in the form selector
- Ensure the passport expiry date is in the future

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues with family profiles:
1. Check that all passport information is complete and accurate
2. Ensure your device has sufficient storage space
3. Try restarting the app
4. If problems persist, the primary profile holder should review family member data for completeness

## Best Practices

### Setup

- **Verify Information**: Double-check all passport details when adding family members
- **Test with Small Trip**: Create a test trip with one destination to verify everything works
- **Regular Updates**: Review and update family profiles periodically

### Travel Preparation

- **Download Forms Early**: Complete country forms well before travel
- **Check Requirements**: Verify each destination's entry requirements for all travelers
- **Backup Strategy**: Take screenshots of completed forms as backup
- **Document Organization**: Use the QR wallet to keep all submission codes organized

### Security Maintenance

- **Regular Reviews**: Periodically review which family members have profiles
- **Remove Unused Profiles**: Delete profiles for people who no longer travel with you
- **Keep App Updated**: Install app updates to get the latest security features

## Advanced Features

### Relationship Management

Family relationships help organize profiles and may influence form requirements:
- **Children**: May have different visa requirements or form sections
- **Spouses**: Often have coordinated travel plans and similar requirements
- **Parents/Siblings**: Useful for extended family trips

### Bulk Operations

- **Multi-Select**: When creating trips, easily select multiple family members
- **Family Templates**: The app remembers common family travel combinations
- **Quick Setup**: Recurring family trips can be created faster using previous selections

### Form Intelligence

- **Smart Defaults**: The app learns from previous family travel patterns
- **Relationship Context**: Some forms may pre-fill based on family relationships
- **Age-Based Fields**: Children's forms may have different default values

## Migration from Single User

If you're upgrading from single-user Borderly to family profiles:

1. **Automatic Migration**: Your existing profile becomes the primary "self" profile
2. **Data Preservation**: All trips, preferences, and QR codes are maintained
3. **Seamless Transition**: Single-traveler features continue to work exactly the same
4. **Progressive Enhancement**: Add family members at your own pace

The family feature is designed to enhance, not replace, the single-traveler experience. You can continue using Borderly exactly as before while adding family members when needed.

---

**Remember**: Family profiles are designed for convenience and security. Each family member's sensitive information is protected with the same level of encryption and privacy as your own profile, ensuring everyone's travel data remains safe and private.
