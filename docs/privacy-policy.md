# Privacy Policy for Borderly

**Effective Date:** March 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 8, 2026

## Overview

Borderly is a privacy-first, local-first travel application designed to help you fill out customs and immigration forms for multiple countries. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

**Key Privacy Principle:** Your passport and personal data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers. Our local-first architecture ensures complete data sovereignty.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

- **Passport Information**: Scanned from your passport's Machine Readable Zone (MRZ), including name, passport number, nationality, date of birth, gender, and expiry date
- **Trip Data**: Travel itineraries, destination countries, and trip dates
- **Form Data**: Completed or partially completed customs and immigration forms
- **QR Codes**: QR codes received after submitting forms to government portals

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect any personally identifiable information on our servers
- We do not track your location or movements
- We do not collect analytics or usage data that could identify you
- We do not have access to your passport data or travel information
- We do not store any data in the cloud or on external servers

## How We Store Your Information

### Local Device Storage

All personal information is stored locally on your device using the following security measures:

- **OS Keychain**: Passport data and encryption keys are stored in your device's secure keychain (iOS Keychain/Android Keystore)
- **Encrypted Database**: Trip and form data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
- **Biometric Protection**: Access to sensitive data requires biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or device passcode)
- **Backup Exclusion**: Sensitive data is excluded from iCloud/Google backups for additional security

### Data Retention

- Your data remains on your device until you explicitly delete it
- Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data
- You can manually clear all data through the app's settings

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

1. **Auto-fill government forms** for customs and immigration declarations
2. **Store QR codes** received after form submissions for easy access
3. **Provide step-by-step guides** for submitting forms to government portals
4. **Maintain trip histories** for your personal reference

## Third-Party Interactions

### Government Portals

When you submit forms, you are interacting directly with government websites (such as Visit Japan Web, Malaysia MDAC, Singapore SG Arrival Card). These interactions are:

- **Direct**: Your device communicates directly with government servers
- **Unmediated**: Borderly does not intercept or process these communications
- **Subject to government privacy policies**: Each government portal has its own privacy policy

### No Third-Party Services

Borderly does not use:

- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Crash reporting services that collect personal data
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services for personal data

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your personal data with anyone.** Since your data never leaves your device, there is no data for us to share.

The only data transmission occurs when you:

1. **Submit forms directly** to government portals (this is between you and the government)
2. **Contact our support** (only the information you choose to include in your message)

## Your Rights and Controls

### Data Access and Control

- **View**: You can view all stored data through the app interface
- **Edit**: You can modify your profile information at any time
- **Delete**: You can delete individual trips, forms, or all data
- **Export**: You can copy form data for manual submission if needed

### Device Security

- **Biometric Lock**: Required for accessing sensitive data
- **App Lock**: Automatic lock after 5 minutes of inactivity
- **Clipboard Security**: Copied passport data is automatically cleared after 60 seconds

## Security Measures

### Technical Safeguards

- **Encryption**: All sensitive data encrypted with AES-256
- **Secure Storage**: iOS Keychain and Android Keystore for maximum security
- **No Network Storage**: Zero-server architecture eliminates data breach risks
- **Code Security**: Regular security audits and updates

### Physical Safeguards

- **Device-Only**: Data cannot be accessed without physical access to your device
- **Biometric Protection**: Requires your biometric authentication
- **Backup Exclusion**: Sensitive data excluded from device backups

## Children's Privacy

Borderly is designed for travelers who possess passports, typically individuals 16 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

## International Data Transfers

Since your data never leaves your device, there are no international data transfers. The only exception is when you directly submit forms to government portals in the countries you're visiting, which is standard for international travel.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

- **Notify users** of significant changes through app notifications
- **Update the effective date** at the top of this policy
- **Maintain transparency** about what information we collect (which is none)

## Legal Compliance

### GDPR Compliance (EU)

Under GDPR, you have rights including:
- **Access**: View personal data (stored locally on your device)
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data (edit in app)
- **Erasure**: Delete personal data (delete in app or uninstall)
- **Portability**: Export your data (copy form data from app)

Since we don't collect personal data on our servers, most GDPR obligations are not applicable.

### CCPA Compliance (California)

Under CCPA, you have rights including:
- **Right to Know**: What personal information is collected (stored locally)
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion (delete in app or uninstall)
- **Right to Opt-Out**: We don't sell personal information
- **Non-Discrimination**: We don't discriminate based on privacy rights

### Other Jurisdictions

We comply with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where Borderly is available. Our zero-collection approach provides strong privacy protection regardless of local regulations.
