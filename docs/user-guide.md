## Getting Started

Borderly is a local-first travel app that helps you auto-generate customs and immigration forms for multiple countries. Your passport data stays on your device, encrypted and secure.

### First Launch

1. **Welcome Screen**: Tap "Get Started" to begin setup
2. **Passport Scan**: Use your camera to scan the Machine Readable Zone (MRZ) at the bottom of your passport photo page
3. **Confirm Profile**: Review and edit the scanned information
4. **Security Setup**: Enable biometric authentication to secure your profile

### Key Features

- **Auto-Fill Forms**: Enter your information once, use it everywhere
- **Smart Delta**: Only shows fields unique to each country
- **Offline QR Wallet**: Store submission QR codes for airport use
- **Step-by-Step Guides**: Follow guided walkthroughs for each government portal

## Setting Up Your Profile

### Scanning Your Passport

The MRZ (Machine Readable Zone) contains two lines of text at the bottom of your passport photo page.

**Scanning Tips:**
- Ensure good lighting
- Hold your phone steady
- Align the MRZ lines within the scanning frame
- If scanning fails, tap "Enter Manually" to input data by hand

**What Gets Extracted:**
- Full name
- Passport number
- Nationality
- Date of birth
- Gender
- Passport expiration date

### Manual Profile Entry

If scanning doesn't work, you can enter your information manually:

1. **Personal Information**: First name, last name, date of birth
2. **Passport Details**: Passport number, nationality, expiration date
3. **Optional Information**: Phone number, email (for some countries)

### Profile Security

Your profile is protected by:
- **Device encryption**: Data stored in your device's secure keychain
- **Biometric lock**: Face ID, Touch ID, or PIN protection
- **No cloud backup**: Your passport data never leaves your device

## Creating and Managing Trips

### Creating a New Trip

1. From the **Trips** tab, tap **"Create Trip"**
2. **Trip Details**:
   - Trip name (e.g., "Asia Travel 2024")
   - Start and end dates
3. **Add Destinations**:
   - Select country from the dropdown
   - Set arrival and departure dates
   - Add multiple countries in order of travel

### Managing Trip Destinations

- **Reorder**: Drag destinations to change travel order
- **Edit**: Tap any destination to modify dates
- **Delete**: Swipe left and tap delete

## Filling Out Country Forms

### Form Generation

When you tap on a country in your trip:

1. **Auto-Fill**: Your profile data automatically fills compatible fields
2. **Smart Delta**: Only empty fields are shown for you to complete
3. **Validation**: Forms validate in real-time as you type

### Form Sections

Each country form is organized into sections:
- **Personal Information** (auto-filled from passport)
- **Travel Details** (auto-filled from trip)
- **Purpose of Visit** (requires your input)
- **Accommodation** (requires your input)
- **Declaration Questions** (requires your input)

### Saving Progress

- Forms auto-save as you complete fields
- Return anytime to continue where you left off
- Green checkmarks indicate completed sections

### Form Completion Stats

Track your progress with real-time stats:
- **Auto-filled**: Fields completed from your profile
- **Completed**: Fields you've filled manually
- **Remaining**: Fields still needing input

## Managing QR Codes

### QR Wallet Overview

The QR Wallet stores all your submission confirmations:
- **Offline Access**: Works without internet
- **Organization**: Filter by country or search by name
- **Quick Access**: Full-screen display for airport scanning

### Adding QR Codes

**From Form Submission:**
1. Complete form submission on government portal
2. Screenshot or copy QR code
3. Return to Borderly and tap "Save QR Code"
4. QR code auto-links to your trip and country

**Manual Entry:**
1. From QR Wallet, tap **"Add QR Code"**
2. **Scan**: Use camera to capture QR code
3. **Manual**: Enter QR data and details by hand
4. Add title, country, and notes

### Using QR Codes at Airports

1. Open Borderly QR Wallet
2. Tap the relevant QR code
3. Tap **"Show QR Code"** for full-screen display
4. Present to immigration officers or e-Gate scanners

### QR Code Organization

- **Search**: Find QR codes by name or country
- **Filter**: Show only codes for specific countries
- **Sort**: By date added or country
- **Delete**: Remove expired or unused codes

## Security and Privacy

### Data Protection

**What Stays on Device:**
- Passport information
- Personal details
- Trip data
- Form responses
- QR codes

**What Never Leaves Your Device:**
- Passport photos
- Passport numbers
- Personal identification data
- Biometric data

### Encryption

- **OS Keychain**: Passport data encrypted with device hardware
- **Database**: Trip and form data encrypted with keys stored in keychain
- **Backups**: Sensitive data excluded from cloud backups

### Access Control

- **Biometric Lock**: Face ID, Touch ID, or device PIN required
- **Auto-Lock**: App locks after 5 minutes of inactivity
- **Clipboard Security**: Copied data clears after 60 seconds

### Data Export and Deletion

**Export Profile:**
1. Go to **Settings > Privacy**
2. Tap **"Export My Data"**
3. Secure export of all trip and form data

**Delete Profile:**
1. Go to **Settings > Privacy**
2. Tap **"Delete All Data"**
3. Confirm to permanently remove all data

## Troubleshooting

### Passport Scanning Issues

**Problem**: Camera won't scan MRZ
**Solutions**:
- Ensure good lighting
- Clean camera lens
- Try different angles
- Use "Enter Manually" if scanning continues to fail

**Problem**: Scanned data is incorrect
**Solutions**:
- Edit information in profile confirmation screen
- Re-scan passport if multiple fields are wrong
- Double-check passport for damage or wear

### Form Issues

**Problem**: Auto-fill not working
**Solutions**:
- Check that profile is complete
- Verify passport data is correctly entered
- Update trip dates and destinations

**Problem**: Form validation errors
**Solutions**:
- Read error messages carefully
- Check date formats (YYYY-MM-DD)
- Ensure all required fields are completed

### QR Code Issues

**Problem**: QR codes not scanning at airport
**Solutions**:
- Increase screen brightness
- Ensure QR code is current and valid
- Try alternative entry methods at immigration

**Problem**: Cannot save QR codes
**Solutions**:
- Check camera permissions
- Try manual entry instead of scanning
- Restart app if camera is unresponsive

### Performance Issues

**Problem**: App running slowly
**Solutions**:
- Close other apps to free memory
- Restart the app
- Update to latest version

**Problem**: Forms taking long to generate
**Solutions**:
- Ensure profile data is complete
- Check device storage space
- Update country schemas if available

### Getting Help

For technical support:
- Check in-app help section
- Visit project documentation
- Report issues through app settings

**Privacy Note**: Support requests should never include passport numbers, personal details, or QR code data.
